About Our Municipality

Discover Bogo City

Bogo is a coastal municipality located in the northern part of Cebu province, Philippines. Originally known as "Bogo," it was elevated to city status in 2007 but reverted to municipality status in 2013 following a legal ruling.

With a rich cultural heritage and strategic location, Bogo serves as a commercial, educational, and healthcare hub for northern Cebu. The municipality is known for its vibrant Pintos Festival, celebrating the local corn-based delicacy.

Today, Bogo continues to develop its infrastructure, tourism potential, and local industries while preserving its cultural identity and natural resources.
